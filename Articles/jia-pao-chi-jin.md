 # 📜 假袍吃金 (jiǎ páo chī jīn)

穿袍未必为公，开口可能为骗  
制服在身，不等于正义随行

📜 本文含中英文版本 / This article contains both English and Chinese versions

骗子很清楚，一身制服，往往比一百句解释更有说服力。

“假袍吃金” 提醒的，是这一层看不见的盲点。  
不是每一个佩章持表的人，都代表公权；    
不是每一句严肃的语气，都有法律依据。

在马来西亚，一名男子在家中遇到两名身穿电力公司制服的人。  
他们一开口就指控他私自改装电表偷电，已经构成刑事罪。

一人拿着表格，一人指向电表，说线路被非法改动。  
他们表示如果正式处理，罚款可能高达两万令吉，甚至会报警。  
但若愿意“私下解决”，只需当场交付五千令吉现金。

男子一下子慌了。  
既怕惹上官司，又见对方态度坚定、衣着整齐，最后还是把积蓄交了出去。

事后才发现，那根本不是电力公司人员。  
只是穿上制服的骗子。

他当时相信的，不是内容，而是那一身衣服。

“假袍吃金” 说的不只是骗术。  
更是我们对外表权威的轻信。

骗局不只存在于短信和网页。  
它也可以走到你家门口，说话得体，穿着正规。

真正的电力公司不会私下收现金。  
真正的稽查人员不会要求现场付款。  
真正的程序，一定留有查证空间。

使用建议:        
遇到上门检查，不要因为对方穿着制服就放下判断。  
先确认身份，再通过官方渠道核实。

# 📜 假袍吃金 (jiǎ páo chī jīn) — False robes consume gold

Scammers understand something simple. A uniform convinces faster than words.

假袍吃金 (jiǎ páo chī jīn) points to this weakness.
Not everyone in uniform carries authority.
Not every firm voice speaks with legal backing.

In Malaysia, a man was approached at home by two individuals dressed as utility company staff.  
They accused him immediately of tampering with his electricity meter and said it was a criminal offence.

One held documents. The other pointed at the meter and claimed illegal modification.  
They warned that penalties could reach RM20,000 and might involve police.  
Then they offered a way out. Pay RM5,000 in cash and the matter would be settled quietly.

He hesitated but fear took over.  
The uniforms looked real. The tone sounded official.  
He paid.

Only later did he realise they were not inspectors.  
They were scammers wearing the part.

What he trusted was not the process.  
It was the uniform.

假袍吃金 (jiǎ páo chī jīn) reminds us that scams do not only happen online.  
They can arrive at your door, properly dressed and confident.

Real utility companies do not collect private cash payments.  
Legitimate inspectors do not demand immediate settlement.  
Genuine cases always allow verification.

Usage application:    
Use this phrase when warning others not to trust appearances blindly.  
It reminds people to pause and verify before acting.
